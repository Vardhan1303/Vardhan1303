## About Me

[cite_start]I am a Mechatronics Engineer specializing in computer vision, deep learning, and autonomous systems[cite: 2, 9]. [cite_start]My expertise lies in designing resilient perception pipelines for safety-critical applications [cite: 9, 18][cite_start], optimizing neural networks for low-latency edge deployment [cite: 9, 18, 38][cite_start], and building advanced synthetic data generation workflows[cite: 9, 23, 38].

* [cite_start]**Perception Optimization:** Proven experience benchmarking and deploying models like PIDNet, DeepLabV3+, and SegFormer [cite: 18][cite_start], dropping real-time inference latency down to 7 ms on edge hardware[cite: 18].
* [cite_start]**Robustness Engineering:** Focused on training models to excel under adverse conditions [cite: 18, 23][cite_start], successfully engineering pipelines that reduced false-positive rates from 4.03% to 0.17%[cite: 18].
* [cite_start]**Synthetic Data Workflows:** Skilled in utilizing mask-guided Generative AI (CycleGAN, VAE) and physics-based simulations to automate dataset generation and remove manual annotation bottlenecks[cite: 18, 23, 38].
